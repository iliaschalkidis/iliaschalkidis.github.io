Ilias Chalkidis

I am PhD student in the Department of Informatics of Athens University of Economics and Business and member of the Natural Language Processing Group. I serve as a director for Nomothesi@ Project developed by Knowledge Representation, Reasoning and Analytics Group in the Department of Informatics of National and Kapodistrian University of Athens.

E-MAIL: ihalk[at]aueb.gr, TEL: +30-6942825565



Research Interests

My general field of research is in Artificial Intelligence, especially Natural Language Processing and Semantic Web Technologies and particularly AI & Law case studies such as:

  • Deep Learning for NLP
  • Legal Text Analytics (Legislation Publishing, Contract Analysis)
  • Information Extraction (Named Entity Recognition and Linking)
  • Text Classification (Document/Sentence Classification)
  • Text Segmentation (Legal Text Segmentation - Zoning)
  • Low-resources Environments (In-domain Transfer Learning)
  • Legal Knowledge Representation (Legislation, Contracts)
  • E-Government – Linked Open Data
