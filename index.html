Ilias Chalkidis

I am a post-doctoral researcher at the Department of Computer Science at University of Copenhagen (CoAStaL NLP Group). I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Informatics at Athens University of Economics and Business. My expertise is in Legal Natural Language Processing (LegalNLP), also known as Legal Intelligence, while I am also actively working in topics related to AI Trustworthiness (Fairness, Robustness, and Explainability), and more recently NLP for politics. I have been a reviewer for *ACL venues and reputable journals, such as Transactions of ACL (TACL), AI & Law (member of the Editorial Board), Nature, Machine Learning, PeerJ, Computer Speech & Language, and Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society. I am also part of the organizing committee of the Natural Legal Language Processing (NLLP) workshop.

Proud father of LegalBERT, a family of legal-oriented language models with 700+ citations up to date, and approx. 7M downloads [1,2] and LexGLUE, a legal NLU benchmark with approx. 276K downloads up-to-date! 🚀

If you find NLP for Law and/or Politics exciting, then please reach out and let me know! 🤗

Research Interests

My general field of research is in Artificial Intelligence, especially Natural Language Processing:

  • NLP for Politics
  • Legal Text Analytics (Legal NLP / NLU)
  • Explainability / Interpretability of NLP models (XAI)
  • AI Trustworthiness (Fairness & Robustness of NLP models)
  • Long Document Processing (Ks of tokens/words)
  • Large-scale Multi-label Text Classification (LMTC)
  • Document to Document Information Retrieval (Doc2Doc IR)
