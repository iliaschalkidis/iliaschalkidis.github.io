Ilias Chalkidis

I am an Assistant Professor of Natural Language Processing (NLP) in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Copenhagen.

My core expertise is in Legal Natural Language Processing (LegalNLP), while I have also worked on topics related to Trustworthy and Responsible AI (Fairness, Robustness, and Explainability). More recently, I am actively working on topics related to NLP for politics, and sociotechnical challenges of GenAI, exploring the political stance of LLMs, and the societal impact of GenAI technologies. I have published several publications, mostly at top-tier conferences, such as venues adjacent to the Association of Computational Linguistics (ACL). My publication record accounts for more than 6,000 citations (Google Scholar). I have also offered invited talks and guest lectures to diverse audiences (CS and Law) in academia and industry. I am also part of the organizing committee of the Natural Legal Language Processing (NLLP) workshop.

If you are excited on topics related to NLP for Politics, or sociotechnical challenges of GenAI, then please reach out and let me know! 🤗
