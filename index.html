Ilias Chalkidis

I am a post-doctoral researcher at the Department of Computer Science at University of Copenhagen (CoAStaL NLP Group). I recently received my Ph.D. from the Department of Informatics at Athens University of Economics and Business. My expertise is in Legal Natural Language Processing (LegalNLP), also known as Legal Intelligence. I have been a reviewer for ACL venues (ACL/EMNLP/NAACL 2020-2021) and reputable journals, such as AI & Law, PeerJ, and Computer Speech & Language. I have also served and currently serve in the program committees of AI and NLP workshops targeting legal applications (AI4LEGAL, NLLP).

If you find Legal Text Processing exciting, then please reach out and let me know! I would love to collaborate in interesting projects 🤗

Research Interests

My general field of research is in Artificial Intelligence, especially Natural Language Processing:

  • Deep Learning
  • Legal Text Analytics (Legal Text Processing / LegalNLP)
  • Information Extraction (Entity Extraction / Sentence Selection)
  • Large-scale Multi-label Text Classification (LMTC)
  • Information Retrieval, especially Document to Document (Doc2Doc) IR
  • Explainability / Interpretability of NLP models
  • Fairness / Robustness of NLP models
  • Low-resource Environments (In-domain Transfer Learning / Few-shot and Zero-shot Learning)
  • Legal Knowledge Representation (Legislation, Contracts)
  • E-Government – Linked Open Data

I mainly study applications of NLP on legal use cases in several sub-fields (e.g., legislation, court cases, contracts).
